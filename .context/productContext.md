# Product Context

## Why this app exists
The WorkToken Platform is built to solve the disparity in institutional workloads. Often, salaried employees take on vastly different amounts of unstructured and ad-hoc tasks, but are compensated identically based on rigid roles. This platform aims to track, tokenize, and cryptographically verify all contributions (both structured like timetables, and unstructured like ad-hoc event management).

## How it works
1. **Users & Roles:** Institutional staff log in securely (via Google OAuth) and are recognized as Faculty, HODs, Directors, or Employees.
2. **Task Pool:** An internal gig-economy board where ad-hoc tasks are posted. Users can self-nominate or be assigned these tasks.
3. **Proof of Work:** Upon completion, cryptographic or file-based proof is uploaded, verified by a supervisor, and approved.
4. **Token Rewarding:** Users earn WorkTokens (WT) for verified completions, providing a transparent, unforgeable audit trail of who is actually doing the work.
5. **Insights:** Dashboards dynamically display live token velocity, active commitments, and individual progress against institutional KPIs.
